About Buckeye PMC

Our purpose and mission.

Our Purpose

The Buckeye Product Management Club aims to equip Ohio State students with the knowledge, skills, and experiences needed to explore and pursue careers in product management. Through speaker events, hands-on workshops, product case studies, and networking with industry professionals, the organization supports students in understanding the intersection of technology, business, and user experience.
